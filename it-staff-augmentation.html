Employer of Record


Employer of Record Services from Merit Cube is a proven, high volume solution for companies who do not wish to incur the cost, effort or risk in temporary or contingent payroll services. Our solutions contain a dedicated Program Management Office (PMO) led by contingent workforce industry veterans who serve as a single point of contact for each client program and end user. The day-to-day operations and end user engagement is supported by the program team and our corporate shared services, including HR, Legal, Payroll, Finance, and Accounting.

Payrolling Solution

Our payrolling solution utilizes the PMO to provide full lifecycle management of each payrolled worker, the PMO is engaged from the initial end user request, to on-boarding and through off-boarding.
Merit Cube has on-boarded thousands of workers throughout the U.S., Canada, and offshore. Our capabilities are not limited by industry.
Our clients select Merit Cube because we understand the importance and value of the payroll population at an effective cost for our clients.
