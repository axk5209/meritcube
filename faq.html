Frequently Asked Questions

My insurance already provides for an annual physical, so why do I need the FirstMD VIP Wellness Program?

The FirstMD VIP wellness program includes an executive physical which allows for you to have an extended appointment that will provide adequate time to spend with your physician. This appointment is complemented with lab test, screening, EKG and diagnostics that goes beyond what is typically included in a basic physical exam. The detailed results of this additional testing, in combination with one-on-one counseling with your doctor, will be used to customize your wellness plan.

Does my insurance cover the annual FirstMD VIP program fee?

No. The annual fee for a membership in an FirstMD VIP-affiliated practice pays for services not typically covered by your insurance and is not reimbursable by insurance.

Can my doctor see me at the hospital?

FirstMD VIP affiliated physicians will coordinate care with the doctors at the hospital and may make courtesy visit. Remember, your physican may not automatically know that you are in the hospital, so please inform him if you are at the hospital.

Can I use my HSA or FSA to pay for the annual FirstMD VIP program Fee?

Yes. After you have received your annual wellness services, the yearly fee may be reimbursed by flexible spending accounts (FSAs), medical savings accounts (MSAs), health reimbursement accounts (HRAs), and health savings accounts (HSAs). If requested, your physician will provide you with a statement of services to present to your administrator as reimbursement may be sought through the FSA claim submission process. Please note that the fee cannot be paid using an FSA debit or credit card.

Do I have to pay the annual fee all at once?

No. In most cases, the annual fee may be paid quarterly, semi-annually or annually, and you may use a major credit card or check. You can also set up convenient automatic payments through a checking or savings account electronic debit option.

Is the annual fee tax deductible?

For patients who itemize medical expenses on their tax returns, the annual fee may be deductible. Check with your tax advisor if you have specific questions

Can I get a refund if I want to cancel my FirstMD VIP membership?

If the FirstMD VIP wellness services were not performed, you may be eligible to receive a prorated refund. If you have received the testing as part of your FirstMD VIP Wellness Program, a refund is not available and any unpaid balance of the annual fee remains your responsibility. As more than 90 percent of patients in FirstMD VIP-affiliated practices around the country renew each year and patient satisfaction is consistently high, we believe you will be very satisfied with the program and your overall healthcare experience.

I have family members who often visit me. Can my doctor see them if needed?

Your doctor may see your out-of-town guests for sick visits and urgent matters. As this may vary, please check with your FirstMD VIP-affiliated physician. Fees, insurance co-pays, coinsurance or deductibles may apply.

What happens when my doctor is away on vacation or otherwise unavailable?

Your doctor will arrange for coverage. You will receive contact information for the covering physician as needed. Additionally, many FirstMD VIP-affiliated physicians will respond to patient messages when they are away.
